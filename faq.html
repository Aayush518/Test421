FAQs

We believe in each student's great potential to achieve dreams through education, confidence, values, and knowledge.

We are located at Kamaladi, Kathmandu (Opposite to National Academy Hall)

You can call the admissions office directly at 9801011824 | 9801012740

The fee structure ranges from Rs 35000 to Rs 38000 excluding transportation. For more information, we request you to visit our school premises.

The school timings will be from 7:40 AM - 3:40 PM, Monday - Friday. The school timings are inclusive of breakfast, lunch, recess, and snacks.

We have an open-door policy for any concern. Formal PTMs are held once every term.

There will be 25 students in each class supervised by two homeroom teachers.

The school curriculum is in alignment and follows the framework of the Nepal Government curriculum with additional subjects and topics. We balance a rigorous comprehensive curriculum with healthy physical, social, and emotional development. A specially curated “Wellness Curriculum” and opportunities for parent-school partnerships ensure the holistic development of all students.

In every term, there is a mix of extracurricular activities and inter-house events.

The school provides students with 3 meals each day - a nutritious and healthy breakfast after arrival at school, lunch, and then snacks before leaving school. All meals are prepared in the school kitchen, served in the cafeteria, and supervised by staff.

Registrations of interest are accepted throughout the year. Formal admission will start in Chaitra 1st week.

Once you register an interest (via the website, phone call, email, or personal visit), the Admissions Office will contact you for a more detailed information-sharing session. ● No fees are charged for admission forms and entrance assessments.
