FAQs

We believe in each student's great potential to achieve Excellence through education, confidence, values, and knowledge.

We are located at Kamaladi, Kathmandu (Opposite to National Academy Hall)

You can call the admissions office directly at 9801011824 | 9801012740

The fee structure ranges from Rs 35000 to Rs 38000 excluding transportation. For more information, we request you to visit our school premises.

The school timings will be from 7:40 AM - 3:40 PM, Monday - Friday. The school timings are inclusive of breakfast, lunch, recess, and snacks.

We have an open-door policy for any concern. Formal PTMs are held once every term.

The teacher-student ratio is 1:20.

The age criteria for admission is as follows:
  • Playgroup: 2.5 years
  • Nursery: 3.5 years
  • KG: 4.5 years

The admission process is as follows:
  • Fill up the admission form
  • Submit the required documents
  • Appear for the entrance test
  • Appear for the interview
  • Pay the admission fee

The documents required for admission are as follows:
  • Birth Certificate
  • Passport size photo
  • Previous school report card (if applicable)
  • Previous school transfer certificate (if applicable)

The medium of instruction is English.
