Our History: Roots in Education

A Legacy of Learning and Leadership

Founded in 2079, following the success of Uniglobe Secondary School/College, Uniglobe School has grown from a vision of educational excellence into a vibrant reality. Our journey is marked by a commitment to holistic education, innovation, and community service, making us a respected and influential institution in Nepal.

Our Mission

"Empowering Students to Excel"

Our mission is to cultivate happy learners, encourage exploration, and emmpower students to excel in their potential. We strive to develop not just academically proficient students but responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

"Inspiring Change Through Education"

We envision a future where education is a transformative force, fostering innovation, empathy, and leadership. Uniglobe School aims to be at the forefront of this change, shaping the leaders and changemarks of tomorrow.

Our Core Values

  • Collaboration
  • Courage
  • Excellence
  • Reflection
  • Responsibility

These values are the cornerstones of our community. They guide our decisions, shape our curriculum, and inspire our students to become the best versions of themselves.
