Facilities

We believe in each student's great potential to achieve dreams through education, confidence, values, and knowledge.

Interactive and Inspiring Learning Spaces

Our classrooms are more than just rooms; they are dynamic learning environments.

Each classroom is equipped with interactive whiteboards, ergonomic furniture, and ample nature light, creating a comfortable and stimulating atmosphere conducive to learning.

Reading Room - A Haven for Book Lovers

The Reading Room is a serene space filled with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources.

It's a perfect spot for students to immerse themselves in literature, conduct research, or simply enjoy the pleasure of reading.

Integrating Technology with Education

Our Digital Hall is at the forefront of educational technology.

Each classroom is equipped with interactive whiteboards, ergonomic furniture, and ample nature light, creating a comfortable and stimulating atmosphere conducive to learning.

Spaces for Physical Activity and Teamwork

Physical education is vital to student well-being.

Our playgrounds are designed for various sports and physical activities, ensuring that students have the space to play, exercise, and learn the value of teamwork and sportsmanship.

Experimenting and Discovering

The Learning Lab is a hub of innovation and exploration.

Equipped with the latest tools and resources, it allows students to engage in hands-on experiments and projects, fostering a love for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

Versatile Space for School Events

Our Multipurpose Hall is a versatile space used for assemblies, performances, and school events.

With modern audio-visual equipment and seating, it serves as a communal area for celebrations, gatherings, and cultural events.
