Required Documents

To ensure a smooth application process, please prepare the following documents:

Prepare Your Application

  • Completed Application Form
  • Copy of Birth Certificate for Age Verification
  • Recent Academic Reports or Transcript
  • Two Passport-sized Photo of the Student
  • Reference Letters(if requried)
  • Parent/Guardian Identification Documents
Note: Additional documents may be required for international students or specific programs.
