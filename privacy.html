Parent Information Session on “Temper Tantrums"

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website: www.uniglobeschool.edu.np are committed to protecting your privacy and take responsibility for the handling of your data very seriously.

About User’s Information

We might automatically gather certain information about you when you visit our website. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, and other technical information. The pages you view, the links you click, and other actions you perform while using our website may also be collected by us. The tracking of your data will be made through Google Analytics.

We will also gather any personal information you voluntarily choose to give us, including your name or email address. When you subscribe to our newsletter or fill out a contact form, for instance, you might provide us with your email address.

Usage of your information

We use the data we gather to deliver and enhance our services, connect with you, and enrich your visit to our website. For instance, we might use your email address to send you newsletters or marketing materials we believe you'll find interesting.

We might also use your information to uphold our policies, enforce the law, or defend our rights and those of others.

Sharing of your Information

Your information might be disclosed to outside service providers who help us run our website, paid- promotions, or offer our services. To send newsletters to our subscribers, for instance, we might employ the services of a third-party email marketing service.

We may also share your information with law enforcement authorities, government agencies, or other third parties if we believe that such disclosure is necessary to comply with the law, protect our rights or the rights of others, or prevent fraud or other illegal activities.

User’s Discretion

You have the option to withhold personal information from us, but doing so might prevent you from using some website features or taking advantage of certain services we offer.

Security

We take precautions to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or obliteration of the data we collect. We cannot, however, guarantee complete security because there is no way to transmit information over the internet or store it electronically that is 100% safe.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time by posting a new version on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our practices.
